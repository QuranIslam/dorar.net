Section 2: The rulings pertaining to entertainment in wedding ceremonies <span class="tip"> [102] It is disheartening that the marital life should start with sins and wrongdoings, most especially among the women, like the use of musical instruments and loud music, inciting dancing, dressing half-naked, staying up through the night thereby leaving obligatory acts of worship. It is obligatory that wedding ceremonies contain no sins and wrongdoings, and only include that which is permissible  so that Allah  puts His blessings into this marriage and unite the spouses in goodness so it can be the start of a good happy life. </span>
